An article about a hedgehog

Title: Little friend of the forest: Interesting facts about hedgehogs Introduction: Hedgehogs are small but very cute creatures that live in our forests and parks. They are known for their charming appearance and funny habits. In this article, we will learn about some interesting facts about these cute creatures.
Solitary hedgehogs Most hedgehogs lead a solitary lifestyle. They prefer to live separately from other members of their species, except for the mating season. Hedgehogs are not afraid to bite Hedgehogs are not the friendliest animals. They can bite if they are scared or cornered. However, this does not mean that they are dangerous to humans. With proper handling, hedgehogs can be very cute and tame. Hedgehogs love milk There is a myth that hedgehogs love milk. In fact, their diet consists mainly of insects, worms and small animals. Milk may not be very good for their health. Hedgehogs sleep in winter Hedgehogs hibernate in winter to survive the cold season. They can sleep for up to six months.
